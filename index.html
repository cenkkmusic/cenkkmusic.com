About

The Story

CENKK is a multidisciplinary electronic artist blending classical training with contemporary club culture. A countertenor trained in opera and classical composition at the Guildhall School of Music & Drama, he integrates precise vocal technique into emotionally charged, atmospheric electronic productions.

His distinctive sound has earned support from leading figures in electronic music including Solomun, Adriatique, CamelPhat, BLOND:ISH, and Innellea. Following a standout collaborative release with Henri Bergmann, CENKK is set to release new music on When Stars Align, Simulate, and Kinetika, alongside previous releases on respected labels such as Multinotes, Borders of Light, and Automatik.

As a live performer, CENKK continues to build a strong international presence with upcoming shows at Village Underground (Fabric) in London, Floyd at Space Miami, Ritter Butzke in Berlin, and E1 London, showcasing his emotionally immersive and vocally driven performances.

Beyond music, CENKK's artistic practice extends into visual and cinematic work. He appeared in the award-winning German short film Icebergs, reinforcing his cross-disciplinary creative identity.

Combining classical discipline with forward-thinking electronic production, CENKK offers a singular artistic profile positioned at the intersection of club culture, contemporary art, and fashion.
